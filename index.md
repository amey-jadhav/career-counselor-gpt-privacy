Privacy Policy for AI Career Counselor & Pathway Coach GPT

This GPT provides AI-powered career counseling and roadmap planning.

Data Usage:

The GPT may process user-provided information such as career goals, learning plans, and scheduling preferences to generate career guidance and calendar reminders.

When calendar integration is enabled, relevant roadmap data (titles and dates) is sent securely to Google Calendar via Make.com solely for event creation.

Data Storage:

This GPT does not permanently store personal data.

No user data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Third-Party Services:

Google Calendar is used only when explicitly requested by the user.

Make.com is used as an automation intermediary.

Contact:
For questions regarding data usage, contact the GPT creator.
